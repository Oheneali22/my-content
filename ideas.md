## Selected Approach: **Approach 2 — Clean Enterprise Tech**

This approach best fits Landmark Technology's brand: professional, trustworthy, and technically credible. It will use Space Grotesk headings, a deep navy hero, electric blue accents, and dark terminal-style code panels.
